Cultural Heritage Background

Jingdezhen blue and white porcelain is an outstanding representative of Chinese traditional porcelain craftsmanship, with a history of over a thousand years. Known for its blue and white colors and exquisite patterns, it is praised as "white as jade, bright as a mirror, thin as paper, and sound like chime." Through blockchain technology, we permanently preserve this precious intangible cultural heritage in digital form, allowing more people to appreciate and collect this artistic treasure.
